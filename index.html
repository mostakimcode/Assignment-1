Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to attend live, or can I watch sessions later?

All 48 technical talks are recorded and added to your dashboard within 24 hours. Pro and Team tickets get lifetime access to recordings; Standard tickets get 30 days.

What do I need to bring for the hands-on workshops?

Bring a laptop with admin/install rights — locked-down corporate machines are the most common reason people lose workshop time. Each workshop lists its specific language and tool requirements on the schedule page two weeks before the event.

Can I transfer my ticket to a colleague?

Yes, up to 7 days before the event, once per ticket, using the link in your confirmation email. After that, transfers close and it's name-badge swaps only at on-site registration.

How does the Team ticket work if we're fewer than 10 people?

Team is a flat $5,999 for up to 10 seats — it doesn't scale down for smaller groups. If you're bringing 3–4 people, compare against Pro at $749 per person before assuming Team is the better deal.

Is there a discount for students or open-source maintainers?

Not currently listed. If you think you may qualify, reach out through the contact form and we'll let you know what's available before you buy a ticket.
